Why Palliative Healthcare?


Doctors: Patients:
We work with you to manage your patients in an efficient way. Using our unique surveying method, any patient can rate their symptoms and leave specific notes to you about how they are feeling. Our system will automatically determine the severity of their illness based on their responses, and within your profile, list all patients in order of symptom severity. This way, you can see which patients have an urgent status. Its that easy! Using our simple unique surveying method, we make it easy to report changes in your symptoms, new symptoms, or new ailments straight to a doctor. No more waiting around in the nurse's office to get your symptoms assessed by the next available staff member. Our survey makes this process streamlined and automatic, and connects you with a doctor sooner. Get in, get out quicker than you ever have before.
